The following are the requirements for the legal assistant project. The project cannot be considered done until all of the requirements are met.

# Features
1. Finds applicable laws for the user's query and street address
2. Displays applicable laws organized by jurisdiction level

# UI
1. Has two inputs: a natural language query and a street address
2. Has a button to submit the query
3. Shows, vertically stacked, all the layers of jurisdiction from the most general to the most specific
4. In each layer, shows the name of the jurisdiction and the applicable laws from it that relate to the user's query

# Backend
1. Applicable laws, by the way they are requested and returned, must conform to a schema that includes all the necessary information to organize them by jurisdiction level
